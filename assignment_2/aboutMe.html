About Me

I have always been an achiever; be it academics or professional life or sports or any other field in my life. I believe in success through hard work and dedication. I like to think about George Bernard Shaw's quote "“Life isn't about finding yourself. Life is about creating yourself.”. I believe that through creativity and hard work, every challenge will be more enjoyable. I enjoy life to the fullest and love humour. I am a progressive thinker and enjoy learning new things. I am a pretty laid‐back person and easy to get along with. I love to play and watch any competitive sports. Being someone who has a passion for sports and loves to exercise, I like to incorprate my workouts using sports as a workout. Whether it is for cardio, strength and endurance, or for simply somthing new to try. I enjoy eating-out, cooking, reading and watching TV series in my spare time.
